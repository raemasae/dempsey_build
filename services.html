WHAT WE DO

Civil Engineers are responsible for much of the infrastructure that we see and use on a daily basis. Public and private roads, as well as everything from airports to tunnels and bridges, are included under this umbrella. In order for a modern society to operate at its best, quality civil engineering skills are essential. Dempsey Pacific, Inc. has a multitude of experience in different areas of civil engineering to make your project move forward efficiently. Please review our services below and contact us to get started. Dempsey Pacific provides civil engineering within the State of Hawaii and throughout the Pacific region.
